# Video Files Directory

## How to Add Your Videos

Place your three MP4 video files in this directory with the following names:

1. **feature1.mp4** - "Everything, Connected" feature video
2. **feature2.mp4** - "Your Place, Remembered" feature video  
3. **feature3.mp4** - "Work That Continues" feature video

## Video Requirements

- **Format**: MP4
- **Recommended aspect ratio**: 16:9 (widescreen)
- **File size**: Keep under 10MB for optimal loading performance
- **Duration**: 5-15 seconds recommended for carousel auto-play

## Video Behavior

All videos will automatically:
- ✅ **Autoplay** when the slide is active
- ✅ **Loop** continuously
- ✅ **Muted** for autoplay browser compatibility
- ✅ **Play inline** on mobile devices

## After Adding Videos

1. Save your MP4 files with the exact names above in this directory
2. Refresh your browser (the dev server should hot-reload automatically)
3. The videos will replace the placeholder images

That's it! Your videos are ready to go.
